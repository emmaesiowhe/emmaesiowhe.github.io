  • General Manager: Oversees day-to-day operations and manages other staff (except the owner)
  • Assistant Manager: Assists the general manager with scheduling, inventory, customer service, and workforce management.
  • Food and Beverage Manager: Manages food and drink preparation, menu development, and dining room operations.
  • Kitchen Manager: Ensures smooth kitchen operations and coordinates with the front of the house.
  • Executive Chef: Develops menus, prepares food, and maintains health and safety standards.
  • Line Cook Prepares food according to recipes and standards.
  • Dishwasher Cleans dishes and kitchen equipment.
  • Prep Cook Assists with food preparation.
  • Sous Chef: Supports the executive chef and manages kitchen staff.
  • Pastry Chef Specializes in desserts and baked goods.
  • Bartender Mixes and serves drinks.
  • Server Takes orders and serves food to guests.
  • Host/Hostess Greets and seats guests.
  • Busser Clears tables and assists servers.
  • Barista Prepares coffee and other beverages.
  • Waitstaff Provides table service.
  • Cashier Handles payments and receipts.
  • Floor Manage Oversees front-of-house operations.
  • Wine Sommelier Recommends wine pairings.
  • Catering Coordinator Manages catering events.
